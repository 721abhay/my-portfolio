Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 28, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Abhay Vishwakarma Portfolio ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our LinkedIn integration feature.

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your LinkedIn account to our portfolio, we collect:

  • Profile Information: Your name and basic profile details
  • Certifications: Your professional certifications and credentials
  • Projects: Your project information and descriptions
  • Access Token: OAuth token for API access (stored locally in your browser)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Display your certifications and projects on your portfolio
  • Sync updates from your LinkedIn profile
  • Provide a personalized portfolio experience
  • Maintain and improve our services

3. Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Your LinkedIn data and access tokens are stored locally in your browser using localStorage
  • No Server Storage: We do not store your LinkedIn data or access tokens on our servers
  • Cache Duration: Data is cached for 24 hours for optimal performance

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute your personal information to third parties. Your data is used solely for displaying your portfolio.

5. LinkedIn API Usage

We use LinkedIn's official API to:

  • Authenticate your account via OAuth 2.0
  • Fetch your certifications and projects
  • Sync updates from your LinkedIn profile

Our use of LinkedIn API is subject to LinkedIn's Privacy Policy and User Agreement.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disconnect: Revoke access to your LinkedIn account at any time
  • Delete: Clear your cached data from browser localStorage
  • Access: View all data we've collected from your LinkedIn profile
  • Control: Choose which information to display on your portfolio

7. Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • OAuth 2.0 authentication protocol
  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Client-side data storage only
  • No server-side storage of sensitive data
  • Token expiration and refresh mechanisms

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use localStorage (not cookies) to:

  • Store your LinkedIn access token
  • Cache your LinkedIn data
  • Remember your sync preferences

9. Third-Party Services

We integrate with:

  • LinkedIn: For authentication and data syncing
  • Vercel/Netlify: For hosting (if deployed)

Each service has its own privacy policy governing their use of your information.

10. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

12. How to Disconnect

To disconnect your LinkedIn account:

  • Clear your browser's localStorage for this site
  • Revoke access in your LinkedIn Settings
  • Contact us to request data deletion

13. Data Retention

  • Active Use: Data is retained in your browser while you use the service
  • After Disconnect: All data is immediately removed from localStorage
  • No Server Retention: We do not retain any data on our servers
