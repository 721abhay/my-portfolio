The long domain you see (e.g., `my-portfolio-git-main-abhay...`) is just the **default preview link**. You can make it much shorter and cleaner for free!

## ğŸ› ï¸ Steps to Change Domain

1.  **Go to your Vercel Dashboard**:
    *   Open your project page on [Vercel.com](https://vercel.com).

2.  **Navigate to Settings**:
    *   Click on the **"Settings"** tab at the top.
    *   Click on **"Domains"** in the left sidebar.

3.  **Edit or Add Domain**:
    *   You will see your current domains listed there.
    *   Type a new, shorter name in the box at the top, for example:
        *   `abhay-portfolio.vercel.app`
        *   `abhay-dev.vercel.app`
        *   `abhay-v.vercel.app`
    *   Click **"Add"**.

4.  **Make it Primary**:
    *   Once added, it might automatically become the main link.
    *   If you see the long one still being used, you can redirect it to the new short one in the configured list.
